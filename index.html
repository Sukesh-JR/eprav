13.9 Crore

There are approximately 13.9 crore internal migrants in India out of which 4 crore have been impacted due to loss of jobs. This accounts to approximately 29% of the total number of migrants. This is the heartbreaking scenario of migrant workers in India.

Skill Upskill Reskill

Development of skills is the need of the hour. If we do not respond to this challenge, we face the prospect of becoming irrelevant. If, as skill professionals, we assume that our old time-served competences will last a lifetime, we will find ourselves becoming candidates for redundancy. The organisations we work for equally run the risk of failing to provide the new products and services that the market requires, resulting in decline. And so, we need to learn continually as we work. This requires a skill set that we need to learn for ourselves as teachers and mentors, and a skill set we need to instil into our learners for their future benefit. Hence having and developing skills is important.
